PCB · Digital electronics · Real-time systems · 2026

128Ch Digital Isolation and LVDS conversion system for custom closed-loop BMI using Intan RHS system

A custom interface board that bridges four Intan RHS 32-channel stimulation/recording headstages to a Speedgoat real-time target machine, providing digital isolation and LVDS signal conversion across 128 channels. Designed to meet the strict timing demands of low-latency, closed-loop brain-machine interfaces where round-trip latency between neural recording and stimulation delivery is critical.

PCB · Analog electronics · 2023

Low-leakage current analog 128-Ch multiplexer for Autolab Potentiostat electrochemical measurements

A custom analog multiplexer board extending an Autolab potentiostat to 128 channels, designed to keep leakage currents negligible for accurate electrochemical impedance measurements.
